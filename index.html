about us


Mount St. Ann High School is an English Medium School Founded in 1981 and named after St. Ann who is a great Saint and Patroness of educators. The School which is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra and affiliated to ‘Central Board of Secondary Education is owned by the Society of Sisters of St. Ann, Province of North India and managed by St. Ann’s Convent, Talegaon Dabhade which belongs to the Christian religious minority group. The Congregation of the Sisters of St. Ann to which the sisters belong is a Christian Religious order founded by the pious couple of Turin (Italy) Carlo Tancredi di Barolo and Giulia Falletti Di Barolo in 1834. Active in the field of education throughout the world since its origin, the Congregation runs in India many Schools, Colleges and various types of training colleges in which thousands of young people, belonging to every social class, community and linguistic group are being educated through the medium of both English and Regional languages.
